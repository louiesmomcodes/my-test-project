# PRD Writer

## Step 0: Confirm intent
Ask: "Ready to write a PRD for [feature]? I'll ask a few questions first."

## Step 1: Collect inputs
- What problem does this solve? (user pain, business outcome)
- Who is the primary user?
- What payer/workflow context applies?
- Any existing research or decisions to reference?
- Target scope: MVP or full vision?

## Step 2: Write the PRD
Structure:
1. Problem statement (2-3 sentences)
2. Goals and success metrics
3. User stories (JTBD format)
4. Requirements (functional, non-functional)
5. Out of scope
6. Open questions

## Guardrails
- Don't make payer-specific assumptions without asking
- Flag dependencies on EHR integrations explicitly
- Keep requirements testable and unambiguous
